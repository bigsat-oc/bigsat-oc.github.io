2026 2016-2025 (BIG Research Day)


Students from the Bioinformatics (BIOF) and Genome Science and Technology (GSAT) graduate programs at the University of British Columbia are pleased to present their 16th Annual Research Day on Thursday, April 16, 2026, at the Life Science Insitute. Over 150 graduate students from both programs will come together to present their research and attend seminars led by invited academic and industry professionals.
